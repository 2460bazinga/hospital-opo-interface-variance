## Data Availability

### ORCHID Dataset

The primary dataset (ORCHID) is available through PhysioNet and requires:

1. Completion of CITI training in human subjects research
2. Signing of a data use agreement
3. Credentialed access approval

**Access:** https://physionet.org/content/orchid/

### OSR 2024 Data

The 2024 validation data is derived from publicly available SRTR OPO-Specific Reports:

**Access:** https://www.srtr.org/reports/opo-specific-reports/
